What it is
resolver is a real-time netflow name resolver. Have a look
What I did
  • Complete design and implementation of system, encompassing the entire software development lifecycle
  • Leveraged knowledge of node-js and asynchronous DNS to perform real-time name resolution of netflow
  • Integrated resolution logs with Splunk for real-time analysis and search
  • Custom splunk app developed to search specific fields and view reports
  • Designed to handle big datasets. 500,000 resolutions a minute; 24+ gigabytes of netflow a day
